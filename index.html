How to use the Pathfinder Web Application
1. What is this application for?
This application is used to visualise the various pathfinding algorithms. It allows you to see how such algorithms plot a path extending from a source cell to a destination cell.
2. How do I use it?
First, choose an algorithm from the dropdown menu (Dijkstra's is selected by default). Next, click on the source button and click on any cell in the grid to place it. Do the same for the destination. You may also choose to place obstacles by clicking and dragging on the grid. When you are done, click the Play Animation button to watch the algorithm run.
3. What are the optional settings?
You may choose to randomly generate obstacles by selecting a maze-generation algorithm from the Maze dropdown menu. You can also customise the theme of the grid by selecting from the Theme dropdown menu.
